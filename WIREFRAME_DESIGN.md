## 🎨 **COMPONENT SPECIFICATIONS**

### **Buttons**

-   **Primary Button**: Gray-700 background, white text, rounded corners
-   **Secondary Button**: Gray-400 background, white text, rounded corners
-   **Danger Button**: Red-600 background, white text, rounded corners
-   **Text Button**: Gray-500 text, no background, underline on hover

### **Cards**

-   **Background**: White (#FFFFFF)
-   **Border**: Gray-200 (#E5E7EB)
-   **Shadow**: Subtle gray shadow
-   **Padding**: 1.5rem (24px)
-   **Border Radius**: 0.5rem (8px)

### **Forms**

-   **Input Fields**: White background, Gray-300 border, Gray-900 text
-   **Labels**: Gray-700, semi-bold
-   **Placeholders**: Gray-400
-   **Focus State**: Gray-500 border, blue accent

### **Navigation**

-   **Header**: Gray-700 background, white text
-   **Sidebar**: Gray-200 background, Gray-700 text
-   **Active State**: Gray-300 background for sidebar items

### **Status Indicators**

-   **Success**: Green dot (🟢)
-   **Warning**: Yellow dot (🟡)
-   **Error**: Red dot (🔴)
-   **Info**: Blue dot (🔵)
-   **Neutral**: White dot (⚪)
